About

Small team.
Senior people.
Real work.

Most data problems aren't really data problems — they're organizational and architectural problems wearing a data costume. Bad pipelines, untrusted metrics, siloed teams, tools bought before anyone thought about how they'd actually be used.

We've been in those situations. We know how to untangle them without blowing up everything that works.

meeha works as an extension of your engineering team — not a vendor that delivers a slide deck and disappears. We write code, review your architecture, sit in your standups, and leave behind systems your team can own and evolve.

Tool-agnostic

What we do

Where we
can help

We work across the full data engineering lifecycle — from early-stage architecture decisions to production-grade systems at scale.

01

Data Architecture & Strategy

Whether you're starting from scratch or inheriting a mess, we help you figure out what to build — and what not to. Lakehouse, warehouse, streaming, hybrid. Designed for where you're going, not just where you are.

Architecture review Roadmapping Tech selection
02

Pipeline Engineering

Reliable, testable, observable pipelines. Batch or streaming. We write production-ready code, not proofs of concept. Everything comes with tests, monitoring, and documentation your team can actually use.

ETL / ELT Streaming Orchestration
03

Analytics Engineering

Raw data transformed into clean, trusted datasets your analysts and stakeholders can build on. dbt models, semantic layers, data contracts — the foundation that makes self-serve analytics actually work.

dbt Semantic layer Data contracts
04

Cloud Data Platform Setup

Standing up a new cloud data platform, or migrating off something that no longer fits. We handle the provisioning, configuration, and optimization — and make sure your team understands what they're running.

AWS GCP Azure
05

Data Quality & Observability

Know when something breaks before your users do. We implement testing frameworks, data quality checks, and monitoring that give your team confidence in what's flowing through the system.

Testing Monitoring Alerting
06

Team Enablement

Sometimes the best thing we can do is make your team not need us. Code reviews, workshops, pair programming, documentation — we're happy to be the last consultants you hire for this problem.

Training Code review Documentation
